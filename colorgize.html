ColorgizeVR

Project Specification

* Awarded Bronze in the VR Experience Category, 2021, at the Animation & Effects Awards(AEAF).

Colorgize VR is a meticulously crafted short experience game for Liminal VR, designed to invigorate users by infusing energy into their virtual world. Players wield electric orbs to imbue vibrant colors and bring the virtual environment to life. This captivating journey features mesmerizing animations, visual effects, and an exclusive soundtrack, delivering a one-of-a-kind audiovisual experience.


Project Brief: Plan and Implement an Energizing VR Game/Experience

Role: Unity Developer

Team Size: 2 Students

Platform: Oculus App Lab, Steam, SideQuest - Liminal VR platform


My contributions

• Enhancing VR grab mechanics through source code modifications

• Managing animations and particle effects within the Unity system

• Designing and implementing satisfying grab, throw, and spawn mechanics tailored to the Oculus Quest controllers

• Developed gameplay features encompassing sound design, user interface enhancements, and fundamental mechanics
