Augemented Reality Kids Game

Galaxy Mates

Project Specification

Galaxy Mates is an engaging AR quiz game designed to educate and entertain children with a focus on science. In this interactive experience, players initiate each question by scanning a captivating galaxy image. Subsequently, they must provide answers by scanning various planet images. Progressing to the next question is contingent upon successfully matching the correct image


Project Brief: Develop an accessible Augmented Reality application tailored for children's learning and enjoyment.

Team Size: 2 Students

Role: Unity Developer

Platform: IOS and Android


My Contributions

• Developed a straightforward C# application within Unity, extracting images from the database and utilizing Vuforia for answer validation

• Crafted easily scannable images of planets to enhance user experience

• Collaborated with designers to compile assets for the creation of a captivating game character responsible for narrating the gameplay

• Diligently sourced high-quality images to facilitate quick and accurate scanning
