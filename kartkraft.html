KartKraft

Project Specification

KartKraft is a kart racing simulation video game. It's designed to provide a realistic kart racing experience, featuring high-quality graphics, physics simulation, and a focus on accurate kart handling and racing mechanics. Players in KartKraft can customize and drive various types of karting vehicles on different tracks, with an emphasis on authenticity in terms of kart setup and handling. The game also includes both single-player and multiplayer modes, allowing players to compete against AI opponents or other players in online races.


Role: Junior Programmer (Gameplay and Scripting Tools)

Platform: Steam

Developer: Motorsport Games


My contributions

• Proficiently programmed in C++ and Lua within the Unreal Engine 4 framework.

• Demonstrated problem-solving prowess through bug fixing across various aspects, including UI, sound, and networking.

• Spearheaded the development of a streamlined game mode and player state management system, enhancing the ease with which designers could introduce new functions to the game's flow.

• Assumed ownership of pivotal gameplay-related tasks, encompassing online player interactions such as joining, leaving, and rejoining sessions, as well as the refinement of the game's user interface, among other responsibilities.
